About Me

I'm 28 years old living in Brooklyn, NY and originally from Boston, MA. Currently studying at Boston University under a Master's of Computer Information Systems program. Professionally, I work as a Solutions Architect at a startup SaaS company called Ordergroove. Ordergroove provides the architecture for ecommerce websites to build and run subscription programs.

Outside of work and school, my hobbies include boxing and rock climbing. I'm a very active person and also do a lot of hiking. My favorite music is a mix of rock and alternative with my favorite bands being Queens of the Stone Age and Tame Impala. My morning drink of choice is always an Iced Chai Latte, sometimes with a shot of espresso.
